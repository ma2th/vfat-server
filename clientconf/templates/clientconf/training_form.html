If the option random training color is checked, the color defined in the field training color (RGB) will be ignored. Instead a random color will be chosen for each new stimulus in order to excite different cone cells.

If the option reinforcement learning is checked, stimuli that were not seen at a certain threshold are re-tested at the same threshold before continuing with an easier threshold. This re-test, however, simultaneously presents the stimulus in the opposite eye in order to give a hint to the brain that there is indeed something perceivable in the training eye.
